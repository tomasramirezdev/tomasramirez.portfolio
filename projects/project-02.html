Key Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Designing for a multi-tenant architecture with diverse client needs.

Solution: Developed a flexible design system with customizable themes and modular components.

Challenge: Optimizing checkout flow for high conversion rates.

Solution: Implemented a streamlined, one-page checkout with guest checkout options and multiple payment gateways.

Challenge: Ensuring mobile-first experience for all client storefronts.

Solution: Adopted a responsive design approach from the start, with extensive testing on various mobile devices.
